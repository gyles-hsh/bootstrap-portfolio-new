About Me

I’m a third-year BS Computer Engineering student at De La Salle University, currently pursuing a minor in Financial Risk and Analytics. I previously served as Vice Director for Student Services in the DLSU Engineering College Government and currently serve as a Vice Chairperson for Enlistment and Officer-in-Charge Chairperson in the DLSU GCOE Enrollment Assistance Task Force.

My technical interests center around PCB and IC design, embedded systems, automation, computer vision, rapid prototyping, and quantitative analytics. I enjoy working at the intersection of hardware and software, building systems from the ground up and transforming ideas into practical, real-world applications.

I’m focused on expanding my skills while exploring how mathematics and machine learning can be applied in financial and data-driven environments.
